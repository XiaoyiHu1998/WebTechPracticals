Our Story

Italian food is a unique and varied cuisine that is enjoyed all over the world. It's most known for its delicious and healthy pasta's and of course the pizza.
But there is much more to be had from antipasti's to seafood and rice dishes, these are the lesser known treasures we want to share to to the world so anyone can have an authentic italian food experience.
Our restaurant opened in 2006 with this goal in mind and has since grown in size and reputation with a michelin star as testament to oud dedication, we invite anyone to come by and enjoy a good meal with friends.

Drinks and Beverages

Italy is well known for its delicious wines, but also has a wide array of refreshing locally popular cocktails.
We offer a wide array of beverages to give you a chance to experience them, of course coffees are also on the menu!

Group Events and Reservations

Want to make sure you can get the seat you want or impress someone? Make a reservation!
We also offer several arrangements from business dinners to private events. Please contact us in advance so we can offer the best experience.
